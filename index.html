L.I.V.E. PVD Screening

Peripheral Vascular Disease (PVD) is a common vascular condition that occurs when veins or arteries are narrowed or blocked, usually due to a buildup of plaque eventually leading to ischemia, or inadequate blood supply, to the limbs and/or organs. The screening involves checking the arteries and veins of your legs for signs of blockages. Results are available immediately.
